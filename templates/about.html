In an ideal world, we would have both:



  Energy efficient homes




  And the means to control their usage.


For most major household appliances, however, it is difficult to impact usage. You can run your washing machine on a cold water cycle to save energy, but its energy usage is primarily determined by the product's fundamental efficiency level. You can adjust the temperature of your refrigerator and freezer, but beyond that, there is no way to impact its usage.





Six million refrigerators are sold every year in the United States.




Refigerators account for 15% of residential electricity use.



Half of purchase decisions are made at point of sale giving sales professionals incredible influence on the consumer's decision and our country's energy use.


Sell efficiency. Efficiently.

sellefficient.ly is an app for sales professionals to sell products of higher efficiency, and in the process earn themselves a higher commission while saving consumers money and making the U.S. more energy efficient.


Our first market opportunity is for appliances sales professionals selling refrigerators with plans to extend to all sales professionals (appliances, cars, homes/contractors) for all energy-related products (lighting, windows, furnaces, water heaters, clothes washers, TV's, etc).
